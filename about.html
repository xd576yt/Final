About Skyfall SMP

Skyfall SMP is a survival multiplayer server focused on community, creativity, and long-term worlds. We keep the experience close to vanilla Minecraft while adding small features that improve gameplay.

What makes us different?

  • No pay-to-win
  • Active moderation
  • Community events
  • Long-lasting worlds
